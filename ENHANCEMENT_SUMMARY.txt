# FINTECH Platform - Summary

**6 Main Sections:**
1. **Home** - Company profile, metrics, recent transactions
2. **Dashboard** - 4 charts, transaction/investment management
3. **Reports** - Financial analytics, salary distribution, recommendations
4. **Shares** - Shareholding patterns, share statistics, price tracking
5. **Contact** - Contact form, organization details, social links
6. **Navigation** - Updated menu structure

**Key Features:**
- Professional dark theme with cyan/blue accents
- 6 chart types (Line, Bar, Doughnut, Radar, Pie)
- Transaction & investment tracking
- Month-wise reporting
- Staff salary calculations
- Form validation
- Responsive design
- Real-time calculations (₹ format)

**Files:** `index.html`, `style.css`, `app.js`, `README.md`

**Status:** ✅ Production-ready with sample data pre-loaded
